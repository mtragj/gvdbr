This easy 15+ mile ride explores the GJ Desert OHV area out of the 27-1/4 Road MX Track, making its way out to the Carpenter Mine.

The route follows open desert two-track and OHV trails at a comfortable pace, a good option for riders looking for an easy day exploring the desert just outside of Grand Junction. All riders are expected to show up with their own dirt bike in working condition and basic riding skills.
