This 20 mile loop follows the Kokopelli 4x4 route through Rabbit Valley. It is a full-width OHV/4x4 trail, making it a great option for riders who want to explore the area at a comfortable pace without committing to the more technical singletrack.

Expect open desert, sandy washes, loose rock, and rolling hills with great views of the Colorado River valley.

<b>Note:</b> This route crosses into Utah, visit this page for Utah Requirements <a href="{{ site.url }}{{ site.baseurl }}/rideutah/">Ride Utah</a>
